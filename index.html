The Gaming Dream

Javelin Polishers was born from a group of people who were desperate to play Dungeons and Dragons regularly. As with most gaming groups life and personal circumstances usually have a knock on effect to a groups success but after a few trial runs and meeting various differnt people. Our little group found each other. We have come along way in the year we have been playing and have lost and added members. But our goals remain the same, being a safe, inclusive space for everyone with an empahsis on fun and friendship.
