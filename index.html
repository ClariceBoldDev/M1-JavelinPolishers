Our History

The gaming group started in July 2022, with a much larger group of people who loved or wanted to learn about Dungeons and Dragons. The group was founded on the principles that the group needed to be a safe space for everyone, including people of colour, those who are neurodivergent, those identifying as LGBTQIA+ and women. All of which can find it hard to find a safe group.

In our early days we were called NPT Gaming Group and within the group lots of different groups emerged. The aim was to form a super group with lots of sessions, however the difficulties in doing so quickly became apparent.

Over the course of a few weeks, we were able to integrate and several of the groups splintered off into smaller groups. Ours was no different, and with a particularly memorable game our group name was formed ‘The Javelin Polishers’. Over the past year we have changed a bit, losing and gaining new members. However, the changes have reflected our need to remain a safe and inclusive space for everyone.

The group have developed friendships and are now looking at expanding from a once-a-week gaming day to craft days, painting sessions and social events; Toby Carvery is a favorite haunt of the Polishers!
